Pigs in Blankets with Maple Syrup

A True Christmas Classic!

Pigs in Blankets: a party essential made even better with maple syrup adding a subtle sweetness.

Ingredients

  • 12 Rashers of smoked bacon
  • A few sprigs of sage, thyme and rosemary
  • 12 chipolata sausages
  • 1 tablespoon (tbsp) Worcestershire Sauce
  • 2 tbsp pure maple syrup

Steps

  1. Pre-heat the oven to 180c/160c Fan/Gas 4.
  2. Lay the bacon rashers down on your workspace, one by one, and stretch the rashers out slighty.
  3. Pull the herb leaves from the sprigs, and sprinkle them over the rashers.
  4. Place one sausage on the end of each rasher, and roll the bacon around it until they're completely wrapped up.
  5. Place in a roasting tin and cook for 20 minutes (or until golden and cooked through)
  6. Add the Worcestershire sauce and maple syrup, and shake the tray to coat the pigs. Loosen the pigs in blankets from the tray if they're stuck.
  7. Heat on the hob and bring to the boil until caramelised and serve immediately.
